BLK x Beetles: A [rough] timeline of my hair growth

Each button corresponds to a year. Press the button to cycle through the months listed. You can only move forward, so keep pressing if you want to see a month again. Each month has a corresponding image and relevant flavor text.

The aim of this work is to celebrate where I’ve been and what I have left to do. I’ve wanted to have some catalog of my loc journey for some time, and have been recording its progress monthly for some time. I figured to combine that with my love for beetles, as they’re noteworthy for rapid growth. And do note that it’s pronounced “Black Beetles”, and not “Black ‘ecks’ Beetles or “Black cross Beetles”. I can’t stop you from mispronouncing it but. C’mon.
